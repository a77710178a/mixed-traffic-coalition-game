## Handoff Notes

- For writing: structure related work around four groups: AIM/signal-free control, mixed-traffic control, game/cooperative right-of-way, risk-aware safety.
- For idea optimization: avoid claiming "first cooperative decision-making method." Claim a narrower conflict-zone coalition game with risk/fairness allocation.
- For direction scouting: the hot zone is real mixed traffic with HDV uncertainty, safety-critical interaction, and social/fair right-of-way.
- For experiment design: include ablations for risk term, fairness term, HDV uncertainty, and local coalition formation.
- For review: deep-read Liu 2026 Part C, Fan 2025 Part C, Lin 2025 TITS, Luo 2026 Transportmetrica B, and Cui 2026 IEEE IoT Journal before freezing novelty claims.
